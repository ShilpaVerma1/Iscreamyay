How it works

iScreamYaY! is a simple app that allows Ice Cream vendors to increase their sales by tracking high-crowd-volume events around town.

Simply download the app.

Create an account or Log in with your Social Media account.

When ready to vend, click High Sales Point button and all the events will be displayed on your map.

Click each event for more info.

Select an event of your choice and drive to the location.

Yes, it’s that simple. And it’s free of charge.
